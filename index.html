Biography

Vinod Bahadur Thapa —
The Man Behind Thapa Technical

From a self-taught developer to one of India's most respected web development educators — the story of Vinod Bhai is an inspiration for every aspiring coder.

Thapa Technical Full-Stack Dev React Expert Next.js MERN Stack YouTuber 8+ Years Experience SEO Specialist

Early Life & Passion for Coding

Vinod Bahadur Thapa was born in Nepal (🇳🇵 5 Jan 1994) and grew up with a deep love for technology and problem-solving. From a young age, he was fascinated by how websites and digital products were built — spending countless hours experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on his own. Unlike many who followed a formal route, Vinod largely self-taught himself the foundations of web development through sheer curiosity and persistence. This hands-on, no-shortcuts approach would later define his teaching philosophy entirely.

Professional Developer Career

Before becoming an educator, Vinod worked as a professional full-stack web developer for over 8 years, building real-world applications for clients and companies. He worked extensively with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) and was an early adopter of Next.js for server-side rendering and SEO-optimised web apps. His industry experience spans e-commerce platforms, admin dashboards, REST APIs, authentication systems, and payment gateway integrations — giving him a uniquely practical perspective that textbooks and bootcamps rarely offer.

Founding Thapa Technical

In the early days of Hindi-medium programming education on YouTube, Vinod noticed a glaring gap — most tutorials were either too shallow or too theoretical, rarely showing how real projects were built from scratch to deployment. So he launched Thapa Technical, a YouTube channel and education platform dedicated to project-based, practical, full-stack development tutorials in Hindi and English. He also founded Kodyfier, an IT institute offering live DSA and web development classes. The channel quickly resonated with lakhs of Indian developers who finally had a teacher who spoke their language — literally and figuratively.

Teaching Philosophy

Vinod Bhai's core belief is simple: "You learn to code by building, not by watching." Every course at Thapa Technical is structured around complete, real-world projects — Netflix clones, job portals, e-commerce stores, blog platforms, and admin dashboards. He emphasises not just writing code that works, but writing code that is clean, scalable, SEO-friendly, and deployable. His teaching style bridges the gap between college education and industry expectations, making students genuinely job-ready rather than just certificate-ready.
